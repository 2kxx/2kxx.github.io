I am an M.S. student at the New Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), and the School of Artificial Intelligence, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS). My research focuses on multimodal large language models (MLLMs) and image quality assessment (IQA), with an emphasis on bridging discrete language-model outputs and continuous perceptual quality scores.

Feel free to reach out via email for collaborations on MLLM-based evaluation, generative model assessment, and trustworthy AIGC.
